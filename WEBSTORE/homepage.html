Pokémon Spotlight

Mewtwo

Mewtwo is a legendary Psychic-type Pokémon. It is extremely rare in Pokémon GO, often appearing in high-level raids and special events. Known for its immense power and psychic abilities.

Rayquaza

Rayquaza is a powerful Dragon/Flying-type Legendary Pokémon in Pokémon GO, known for its immense strength in raids. It's famous for its role in calming the conflicts between Kyogre and Groudon.

Charizard

Charizard is a Fire/Flying-type Pokémon. While not as rare as legendaries, Charizard is still highly sought after. It can be obtained through evolution or in specific raid battles, and is known for its powerful fire attacks.
